FROM MY JOURNEY… SO FAR.

2001 – 2020

At an early age, I developed a passion for design, often drawing inspiration from my father, who is really skilled at drawing. I would watch him sketch and create amazing art, and I felt motivated to imitate his style. Drawing cartoons and anime became a daily routine, and it sparked a deep creative drive in me. His influence was a big part of my childhood creativity, and it fueled my desire to learn and experiment with different forms of art. As I continued to draw, I started experimenting with different techniques and mediums. I won small design competitions and worked on personal projects, which helped me develop an eye for aesthetics, composition, and storytelling. These experiences taught me the importance of attention to detail and how to communicate ideas visually.

My passion for art led me to explore various creative fields, and I eventually decided to pursue ICT animation. This was a pivotal moment in my journey, as it allowed me to develop my skills in digital art, 3D modeling, and animation. The skills I gained during this time not only gave me a strong technical foundation but also taught me how to think critically and approach design with a problem-solving mindset. In 2021, I had the chance to apply everything I had learned during my time in ICT animation in an immersion program. This opportunity allowed me to work on real-world projects and collaborate with other talented individuals. My work was even featured in an international conference, which was a huge milestone for me. It was a validation of all the hard work I had put into refining my skills, and it motivated me to continue pushing the boundaries of my creativity.

Looking back, my journey from childhood doodles to professional work in animation has been incredibly rewarding. Each experience has contributed to my growth as a designer, and I’m excited to see where this journey will take me next. I’m always eager to learn more and explore new creative possibilities.

2021 – Present

Today, my work spans across 3D visualization, illustration, and branding. I focus on bringing ideas to life through detailed renders, dynamic compositions, and storytelling. Over the years, I have honed my craft to ensure that every design I create not only looks appealing but also effectively communicates the vision behind it. I've worked on a variety of projects, from game concept art to commercial branding, collaborating with diverse clients and businesses. These experiences have helped me refine my skills, learning to tailor designs to meet specific needs while pushing creative boundaries. My goal is to create designs that not only look good but also communicate a strong visual identity that resonates with the target audience.

Whether it’s 3D modeling, illustrations, or branding, I aim to push creative boundaries and bring unique ideas to life. My work is driven by a deep passion for innovation and a commitment to creating impactful, visually stunning designs. I strive to stay ahead of design trends and continuously explore new techniques and technologies to elevate my projects. In addition to my professional work, I’ve taken part in various immersive programs, workshops, and industry conferences. These opportunities have allowed me to network with like-minded creators and stay updated on the latest developments in the design and animation world. Every project I take on is an opportunity to learn, grow, and deliver work that I can be truly proud of.

As I continue to develop my skills, I am always eager to explore new areas of design, collaborate with new clients, and tackle fresh challenges. The journey from learning the basics of digital art to mastering complex 3D visualizations has been incredibly fulfilling, and I look forward to the many opportunities that lie ahead.
